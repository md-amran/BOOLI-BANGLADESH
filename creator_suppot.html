Frequently Asked

🔹 How do I make a call on Booli?

Open any chat → tap phone (audio) or video icon → call connects instantly using Agora HD engine.

🔹 Is my data safe?

Yes! Booli uses Firebase security rules, encrypted database, and optional disappearing messages.

🔹 Can I use Booli on multiple devices?

Currently web version works across browsers. Mobile apps coming soon.

🔹 I forgot my username/password.

Use "Forgot password?" on login screen. For username recovery, click "Forgot ID?" — we'll send details to your email.

🔹 How to report a bug or abusive user?

Use the in-app report button or email us at support@booli.app.
